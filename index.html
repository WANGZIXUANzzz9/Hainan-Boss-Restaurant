A BRIEF INTRODUCTION TO HAINAN CUSTOMS

Hainan is a contential island. It's the only province in China that is completely tropical. Therefore, a very distinctive food culture has been formed. Hainan has 'four famous dishes' and 'four famous noodles' and is also rich in coffee and tropical fruits like coconut. Besides, Hainan is also famous for its Miao cuisine.
